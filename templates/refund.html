Cancellation, Refund and Withdrawal Policy

Last Updated: July 17, 2026

1. General Return/Refund Policy

1.1. CLASSIC REFUNDS (CHARGING BACK COMPLETED DEPOSITS DIRECTLY TO CARDS/WALLETS) ARE NOT PROVIDED UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES.

1.2. Once a deposit is successfully processed, the funds are instantly converted into the platform's virtual currency to be used for matchmaking or store purchases. To retrieve these funds, the User must utilize the standard Withdrawal system as described in Section 2.

1.3. For intangible digital products (such as a 30-day Premium Subscription), once activated, the service is deemed fully rendered and is completely non-refundable.

2. Withdrawing Virtual Account Balances (Payout Rules)

2.1. Users can request a withdrawal of their available (unfrozen) virtual platform balance to their external payment methods (crypto wallets, e-wallets, or cards) through the "Withdraw" panel in their profile.

2.2. The strict minimum withdrawal amount is $5.00. Any payout request below this threshold will be automatically rejected by the system.

2.3. All withdrawal requests are subject to manual moderation to prevent fraud and cheating. Processing of requests takes up to 24 hours.

3. Transaction & Payment Gateway Fees

3.1. ALL TRANSACTION FEES, INCL. DEPOSIT PROCESSING FEES AND WITHDRAWAL GATEWAY/GAS FEES, ARE COVERED SOLELY BY THE USER.

3.2. These fees are deducted directly from the transaction amount at the moment of payment or payout processing. The Platform does not charge or absorb any payment merchant processing fees. The exact fee depends on the chosen payment method (crypto network fees, bank card processing rates, etc.).

4. Duel Lobby Cancellation & Asset Release

If a User initiates a Matchmaking Duel Lobby, and no active challenger connects, or if the creator chooses to close the lobby prior to the match start, all escrowed ledger funds are immediately released and refunded to the User's primary virtual balance in full (100% refund, 0% platform fee).

5. Dispute Settlement

If you experience transaction errors, double-billing, or system latency during top-ups, immediately notify our support team: support@likeagod.net.
