Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To ensure secure operations and verify competitive match results, the Platform utilizes the secure Valve Steam OpenID authentication protocol. We collect and process only public profiles metadata:
- Unique SteamID64 identifier;
- Steam profile avatar URL;
- Public user nickname.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

All gathered user metadata is strictly processed for identity authentication, custom matchmaking ranking calculations, competitive duel records logging, transaction safety, and display customization within the interactive match lobby rooms.

3. Information Security

The Service Provider maintains robust administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect user profile logs and financial operations against unauthorized access, alterations, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Deletion and Support Requests

If you wish to terminate your account and request complete database deletion of your user profile, contact our compliance team at: @likeagod_support.
